Planet Name & M/D/G Fun Facts Roman / Greek God
Mercury
Mass: 0.0553
Density: 0.984
Gravity: 0.378
  • Mercury doesn't have any moons (neither does Venus).
  • Mercury is the fastest planet, orbiting the sun every 88 Earth days.
Mercury / Hermes
Venus
Mass: 0.815
Density: 0.951
Gravity: 0.907
  • Venus is the third brightest object in the Earth's sky.
  • Venus has a runaway greenhouse effect. It's so hot that rocks melt.
Venus / Aphrodite
Earth
Mass: 1
Density: 1
Gravity: 1
  • Coral reefs are Earth's largest living structure (though climate change is killing them).
  • Earth's magnetic poles are slowly moving westward.
Tellus / Gaia
Mars
Mass: 0.107
Density: 0.713
Gravity: 0.377
  • Mars has 37% less gravity than Earth, so you could jump three times higher on Mars.
  • Mars has two moons, Phobos and Deimos, which are thought to be captured asteroids.
Mars / Ares
